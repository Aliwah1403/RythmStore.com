About Us

First opened in the late 1990's, Rythm House is located in the midst of the busy streets of Mumbai city. As our name suggests, our sole purpose has and always will be to cater for everyone's music needs regardless of their age; Be it 70's rock albums, pop music, childrens' poems or devotional music we have it all. Moreover, we're known as the only music store to host concerts and live shows which we tend to do very often. We are more than happy to cater for your needs!
